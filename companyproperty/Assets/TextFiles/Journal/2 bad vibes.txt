People rarely talk to each other here. No social hangouts, short lunch breaks. Everyone sits in silence, working diligently like little ants, supervisors at every corner, uncomfortable amounts of cameras staring us down. 

It's a decent paying job... but I won't lie, I don't like the vibes. I'll just try to fit in and do what's expected of me, and not think too much about any of this...
